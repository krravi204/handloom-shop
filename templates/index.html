The {{ store_settings.store_name.split(' ')[0] }} Philosophy

Embracing Slow, Sustainable Fashion

{{ store_settings.store_name }} was born out of a desire to protect India’s vanishing textile crafts and elevate the livelihoods of traditional artisans. Every garment in our store tells a story: from the cotton harvested in organic fields, to natural indigo vats, to the rhythmic clicking of the loom.

By bypassing middle-men, we ensure that over 75% of the sales price goes directly back to the weaver collectives. In buying a {{ store_settings.store_name.split(' ')[0] }} product, you are not just acquiring a premium garment, but you are carrying forward a centuries-old cultural heritage.
